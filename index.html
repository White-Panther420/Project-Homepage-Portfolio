One Piece Battleship

Am HTML, CSS, and JavaScript-based project where you can face off against the infamous Admiral Akainu as Monkey D. Luffy! The game features audio and images to give it an interactive feeling, as well as a point system to keep track of the number of ships remaining on the board.

Weather App

The weather app displays an overview of the weather in a given city location. This includes things like temperature, cloudiness, UV Index, wind direction, etc; You can also view a 3-day forecast of the weather.

To Do List App
